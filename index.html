CID KAGINOU

Cid Kagenou (シド・カゲノー, Shido Kagenō?), formerly Minoru Kageno (影野実, Kageno Minoru?), is the titular antihero and protagonist of The Eminence in Shadow series. After a premature death, he reincarnates into a world of swords and sorcery. Choosing Shadow as his nom de guerre, he leads the secret organization Shadow Garden. He lives out unfulfilled Chunibyou (厨二病, Chūnibyō?) fantasies of being an enigmatic shadow broker, unaware that his creative scenarios are close to the truth.

SERVICES

Martial Art

Hand-to-hand martial arts are another victim of the new world's unrefined teachings. It is regarded as advantageous to practice magic, as throws and guards can be made irrelevant via superior magical force.

Magic

Magic is essential for fights in this new world. Cid describes magical energy as "minuscule grains of light floating" in his body. His magic differentiates from typical dark knights as the color manifests as shades of blue and purple.

Nuclear Magic

Cid's signature nuclear or atomic magic is the culmination of his magical research and training; it is the most potent technique in his repertoire. The functional principle behind it is the accumulation and compression of magic into a single point, his sword, followed by release.

Physical Capabilities

Magical reinforcement or augmentation assists Cid in surpassing the limits of the average human body and many dark knights. One documented numerical value of Cid's speed is 1,200 km/h (648 kn 746 mi/h).

Intelligence

Cid shows a higher-than-average intelligence in several areas but falls below in others. He has noteworthy fluid intelligence in battle. As a combat savant, tactical decisions and calm analysis assist his methodical approach to fighting.

Normie Techniques

Mob Cid keeps a low profile to dissimulate his "true" shadow broker identity. Mob Cid is a mediocre individual who blends into the background. Some actions and words are carefully crafted to reinforce this perception.
